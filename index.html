What is Plandria?

MADE FOR
THE PLANET

Plandria ensures flowerpots and containers are made with better, reusable materials that aren't harmful to the environment.

  • Concrete base — when fresh, concrete absorbs carbon dioxide, making it an active eco material.
  • Natural wax — used by farmers to protect plants from drought, UV damage, and pests.
  • Two life cycles — candle first, then flowerpot, pencil holder, or whatever you need.
  • Eco & frugal — designed for those who care about the planet and love getting more from what they buy.
How It's Made

THE PROCESS
BEHIND THE POT

01

Mortar Base

Each pot starts with a mortar base — durable, strong, and coated in a glaze to eliminate dust residue.

02

Dust Dye Colour

Dust dye is measured and mixed into the liquid mould before casting — giving each pot its unique, even colour.

03

Braided Wick

Three cotton wicks are braided together by hand, creating a consistent, clean-burning wick for the natural wax.

04

Natural Wax Pour

Natural wax is poured into the finished pot — the same wax farmers use to protect plants. Clean, sustainable, calming.
